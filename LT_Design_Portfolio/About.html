Laina Terpstra is a Brooklyn based painter, designer, and web developer. Her instincts as an artist deeply inform her design work which favors a clean, dynamic, elegant aesthetic. She has experience designing and constructing custom websites, logos, and illustrations.

Laina studied Fine Art at Sarah Lawrence College, and Front End Web Development at General Assembly, NY. Laina has worked with other esteemed design studios such as JLF Studio in Brooklyn, NY, as well as the award-winning design company, Terpstra Design, in San Francisco.
