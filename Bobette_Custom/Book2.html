DO STORY: HOW TO TELL YOUR STORY SO THE WORLD LISTENS

In her first book, Bobette reveals her 10 principles of storytelling, such as juxtaposition and the 'gleaming detail', and demonstrates how they can be incorporated into your own storytelling — whether that's developing a film script or literary work, putting together a pitch or writing your company's 'About' page — to advance the narrative, create an emotional connection and ultimately turn your story from good... to great.   So, what's your story?
