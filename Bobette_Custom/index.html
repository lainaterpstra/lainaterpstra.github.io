Bobette Buster
STORY GURU   SCREENWRITER
DOCUMENTARY   &   FEATURE FILM PRODUCER
ADJ. PROFESSOR   CONSULTANT

 

Over 25 years experience as a graduate (MFA) and Ad. Professor at the University of Southern California's School of Cinematic Arts Peter Stark Program, and as a Hollywood producer, creative executive for Tony Scott, Larry Gellbart and Ray Stark, Bobette continues to consult for Hollywood's leading decision makers and creatives at Pixar, 21st Century Fox, Disney Animation, and Sony Animation. She also conducts seminars for the most elite film school programs around the world as well as in-depth, immersive workshops on how to be a storyteller for corporate and not-for-profit organizations, advertising and idea conferences.

 

Bobette is the author of: Do Story: How to tell your story so the world listens.

 

"Bobette Buster's classes have been a wonderful addition to our internal education program. Her insights and thorouhness in examining every aspect of a film have been immensely valuable to our ongoing commitment to learning and creativity."

— Elyse Klaidman, Director, Pixar Studios University
